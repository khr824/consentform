Research Consent & Release Forms

Comprehensive checklists and generic templates for qualitative research, oral consent, and performance releases.

Structure of a Qualitative Consent Form

A standard qualitative consent form should guide the participant logically through the study details. Below is the general structure and a checklist of what to include.

General Structure

  • Heading & Introduction: Study title, researcher information, and a brief invitation to participate.
  • The "Why" and "What": The purpose of the study and the specific procedures the participant will undergo.
  • Risks, Benefits, and Compensation: Any emotional/privacy risks, societal benefits, and payment details.
  • Data Management: How confidentiality will be maintained and data protected.
  • Conclusion & Signatures: Contact information, a statement of voluntary consent, and signature lines.

Checklist: What to Include for Qualitative Research

  • Explicit consent for audio/video recording.
  • Clear explanation of how direct quotes will be used (e.g., with pseudonyms).
  • Details on data retention and destruction (e.g., when recordings will be deleted).
  • A statement emphasizing the right to skip any questions without penalty.
  • Clear boundaries on the limits of confidentiality (e.g., mandatory reporting).
  • For focus groups: A clause asking participants to respect others' privacy, noting the researcher cannot guarantee it.

Oral Consent Guidelines & Template

For protocols involving oral consent, a conversational script is often more appropriate than a standard template.

Oral Consent Checklist

  • Introduction detailing who the interviewer is, their affiliation, and organization.
  • A statement clarifying that the study involves research.
  • The purpose of the study.
  • What the participant will be asked to do and the time commitment, including follow-ups.
  • Compensation details and any information needed to process payment (like an email or mailing address).
  • The voluntary nature of participation.
  • Risks or benefits associated with participating (omit if there are none).
  • Notification that you are taking notes or recording data.
  • Whether the collected information will remain confidential or if identifiers will be kept with data.
  • Contact information for the researcher and/or the IRB.
  • An opportunity for the participant to ask questions.
  • An explicit question asking if they agree to participate, with a record of their response.

Sample Oral Consent Script

Hello, my name is [Name] and I am a researcher with [Organization/Affiliation]. I am inviting you to participate in a research study designed to [briefly state study purpose]. If you agree, I will ask you some questions about [topic], which will take approximately [amount of time]. [Optional: You will receive [Compensation] for your time, and I will need your email address to send it]. Your participation is entirely voluntary. [Optional: There are no expected risks or direct benefits to you]. I will be [taking notes / recording our conversation]. Your answers will remain confidential and your name will not be linked to your responses. If you have questions later, you can contact me at [Phone/Email] or the Institutional Review Board (IRB) at [IRB Contact]. Do you have any questions for me right now? Do you agree to participate in this research? [Record response: Yes/No]

Adult Performance Release Form

Adult Consent Checklist

  • Acknowledgment that the specific department/division is making media including the participant's name, likeness, and voice.
  • Granting the institution the right to make, use, and publish recorded footage across various media formats.
  • Permission to edit, mix, duplicate, and reuse recordings without restriction regarding changes or alterations.
  • Statement establishing the institution's complete ownership of the recordings.
  • Granting the right to distribute, broadcast, and exhibit the recordings for commercial or non-commercial purposes.
  • Waiving any right to inspect or approve the finished product.
  • Releasing the institution from any claim or liability, including defamation or copyright infringement.
  • Confirmation that the signer is over eighteen and understands the release.
  • Spaces for signature, date, name, and address.

PERFORMANCE AGREEMENT AND RELEASE 1. I, _______________, have been informed and understand that [Researcher Name] in the [Department/Division] of [Institution Name] is making a [type(s) of media used] in which my name, likeness, image, and/or voice may be included. 2. Minor Performance Release Form

Minor Consent Checklist

  • Statement that the signer is entering the agreement on behalf of their minor child.
  • Permission granted for the institution to make, use, and publish recorded footage of the minor.
  • Right to edit, mix, duplicate, and reuse recordings without restriction.
  • Confirmation that the institution has complete ownership of the programs featuring the minor.
  • Right to broadcast, exhibit, market, and distribute recordings for commercial, non-commercial, or legal purposes.
  • Waiver of the right for the parent or minor to inspect or approve the finished product.
  • Release of the institution from liability, including defamation or copyright infringement.
  • Understanding that the minor will not be named in the work's credits.
  • Warranty that the signer is over eighteen and has the legal right to contract for the minor.
  • Signature lines for mother/father/guardian, date, printed name, and address.

Sample Minor Release Form

MINOR RELEASE I, _________________________, enter into this agreement with [Institution Name] on behalf of my minor son/daughter, _______________________________. I have been informed and understand that the __________________________ [Department or Division] of [Institution Name] is making a ____________________ [video/media type] in which my son/daughter's likeness, image and/or voice may be included. I hereby grant my permission for [Institution Name] and its employees and agents to make, use and publish any recorded footage of my son/daughter in whole or in part, whether recorded on or transferred to videotape, film, slides, photographs, audio tapes or other media. This includes, without limitation, the right to edit, mix or duplicate, and to use or reuse recordings in which he/she has participated, in whole or in part, without restriction as to changes or alterations. [Institution Name] shall have complete ownership of the program or programs in which he/she or his/her performance or contribution appears. I also grant [Institution Name] the right to broadcast, exhibit, market, and distribute any of said recordings, either alone or as part of a finished production; for commercial or non-commercial television, theater, closed-circuit exhibition or any other legal purposes as [Institution Name] or its employees and agents may determine. This includes the right to use said recordings for promotion or publicizing any of these uses. I hereby waive any and all right that I or the minor may have to inspect or approve the finished product or printed matter that may be used in connection therewith. I expressly release [Institution Name] and all persons acting under its permission or authority or those for whom it is acting, from any claim or liability arising out of or in any way connected with the above uses and representations including any and all claims for defamation or copyright infringement. I understand that the minor will not be named in the credits of the work. I hereby warrant that I am over the age of eighteen and have every right to contract for my minor son/daughter in the above regard. I state further that I have read the above release and fully understand its contents. date: ____________________________ signature: mother/father/guardian _______________________________________ printed name _______________________________________ address ____________________________________________________________

Parent/Guardian Permission (Opt-Out Focus)

In most cases, active (opt-in) consent is required. However, in limited cases with no risk, the IRB may allow passive (opt-out) consent.

Parent/Guardian Consent Checklist (Opt-Out Model)

  • Principal Investigator, Faculty Advisor, and Study Title.
  • A clear explanation of the research activities and the expected duration of the child's participation.
  • Confirmation of whether identifying information will be collected (or if it is anonymous).
  • A statement on the risks and benefits to the child.
  • Contact information for the research team to answer questions and the IRB to report concerns.
  • Instructions providing multiple ways for a parent/guardian to inform researchers they do not want their child to participate (e.g., returning a form or emailing).
  • A due date giving parents sufficient time (at least a week) to review and act.
  • A "Notification of Refusal" section with spaces for the child's name, parent's signature, printed name, and date.

Sample Parent/Guardian Opt-Out Notification

Sample Parental Notification Form Regarding Participation in a Research Study Principal Investigator: [Principal Investigator Name] Faculty Advisor: [Faculty Advisor Name] Study Title: [Study Title] Dear Parents and Guardians, I am a [Title/Role] at [Institution Name]. I am conducting a research study of students to [briefly state purpose of research]. [School Name] is familiar with and has given me permission to conduct this research at the school, and is facilitating this communication to you to tell you about the study and give you an opportunity to decide that you do not want your child to participate, if that is your preference. If you allow your child to participate, we will ask them to [describe activities, e.g., complete two short surveys]. Each activity will take no more than [amount of time], and students will not miss any instruction time in order to participate. There are no known risks to your child from participating in this study. Their grades and class standing will not be affected in any way if they do, or do not, participate. This research is anonymous. No names or other identifying information will be collected. Please contact [Principal Investigator Name] at [Email Address], or [Phone Number]. If you wish to share a concern or complaint, please contact the Institutional Review Board (IRB) at [IRB Email Address], or [IRB Phone Number]. If you do want your child to participate, no further action is necessary. If you do not wish your child to participate, please fill out and sign the form below and return it to your child's teacher or to [Principal/Administrator Name]. Alternatively, you can send an email to the researchers with the subject line "Opt Out", and include your name and your child's name in the message. Please return the form on the next page or reply via email by [Date]. --------------------------------------------------------- Notification of Refusal: I DO NOT give permission for my child to participate in the study described above.
